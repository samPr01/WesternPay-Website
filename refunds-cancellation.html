Refunds & Cancellation Policy

Understand our refund policies and cancellation procedures for all WesternPay services.

Last updated: December 15, 2024

Money Transfers

Domestic and international money transfer refund policies

  • Cancellation within 30 minutes of initiation: Full refund
  • After 30 minutes but before processing: Partial refund (minus processing fees)
  • After processing: Refund depends on recipient bank policies
  • Failed transfers: Automatic refund within 3-5 business days

Bill Payments

Utility and service bill payment refund policies

  • Successful payments: No refunds (payments are final)
  • Failed payments: Automatic refund within 24-48 hours
  • Duplicate payments: Refund of duplicate amount within 3-5 days
  • Wrong biller payments: Case-by-case review required

Mobile Recharge

Mobile recharge and DTH payment refund policies

  • Successful recharges: No refunds (recharges are final)
  • Failed recharges: Automatic refund within 2-4 hours
  • Wrong number recharges: No refunds (user responsibility)
  • Operator issues: Refund processed after operator confirmation

Business Services

API and business account refund policies

  • Setup fees: Non-refundable after service activation
  • Monthly subscriptions: Prorated refunds for unused periods
  • Transaction fees: Non-refundable for completed transactions
  • Unused API credits: Refundable within 90 days

Cancellation Process

Initiate Cancellation

Log into your account and navigate to the transaction you wish to cancel. Click on the 'Cancel' button if available within the allowed time window.

Verification

Confirm your identity through our security verification process. This may include entering your password, PIN, or two-factor authentication code.

Review Cancellation

Review the cancellation details including any applicable fees or penalties. Confirm that you want to proceed with the cancellation.

Processing

Your cancellation request will be processed immediately if within the allowed timeframe. You'll receive a confirmation email and SMS notification.

Refund Processing

If applicable, your refund will be processed back to the original payment method within the specified timeframe for your service type.

Important Terms

Refund Processing Times

  • Instant refunds: Credit cards and UPI (for eligible transactions)
  • 1-3 business days: Bank transfers and wallets
  • 3-7 business days: International transfers and complex disputes

Non-Refundable Items

  • Completed and delivered services
  • Transaction fees for successful operations
  • Third-party charges (bank fees, conversion fees)
  • Services explicitly marked as non-refundable

Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision, you can escalate your case through our dispute resolution process. We aim to resolve all disputes within 15 business days.

Contact Support

For refund requests or cancellation assistance, please contact our support team:

Refund Support

  • Email: refunds@westernpay.in
  • Phone: 1800-WESTERN (24/7 support)
  • Live Chat: Available on our website
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours for refund requests

What to include in your refund request:

  • Transaction ID or reference number
  • Date and time of transaction
  • Amount involved
  • Reason for refund request
  • Supporting documentation (if applicable)
