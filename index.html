About WesternPay

WesternPay is building the connective tissue for digital money movement—serving individuals, startups, and enterprises. Our mission blends financial accessibility with developer-grade tools: instant domestic rails, intelligent bill and utility workflows, intuitive recharges, and open fintech APIs that unlock new business models. Backed by insights-driven analytics and compliant global corridors, we help you move money confidently—across India and across borders.

From IMPS, UPI, and NEFT to cross-border remittances, WesternPay combines speed, security, and scale. Whether you’re streamlining consumer payments or embedding financial services in your products, our platform adapts to your ambition.

Build with simplicity. Launch with confidence. Grow without limits. WesternPay is your partner for domestic and global payments, now in 190+ countries.
